---

![](/banner.png)

The AnvilCraft mod is centered around effects triggered by anvils falling and striking blocks, items, or entities.

Placing different blocks beneath the anvil activates various effects.

It facilitates automation processes that are difficult to achieve in vanilla gameplay.

Certain non-renewable items in vanilla can be regenerated through this mod.

To simplify lifting anvils, magnets have been added—though this function can technically be replaced by vanilla redstone
machinery, it is much more cumbersome.

The mod's late-game progression includes unique technological elements.

It raises the limits of vanilla enchantments and introduces gameplay with magical aspects.
